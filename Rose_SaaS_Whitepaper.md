## 1. Overview

RoseAI Essential is a one-stop digital platform purpose-built for small to medium-sized restaurants across Canada. Powered by Cloudflare's global edge network, it delivers custom restaurant websites, QR code ordering, staff management dashboards, AI-powered customer service (chat + optional voice), RAG knowledge base Q&A, and multi-channel payment integration — all for a single one-time build fee.

The system uses a four-tier product architecture (Essential Basic / Standard / Premium / Custom), allowing restaurants to start at the level that fits their needs and upgrade seamlessly as they grow — from a simple branded website all the way to full-stack intelligent operations with AI and payments.

**Our mission:** Give every small Canadian restaurant enterprise-grade digital capabilities at an affordable, one-time price — with zero technical skills required.

**Service model:** We charge a one-time system build and deployment fee. Monthly cloud operating costs are paid directly by the merchant to third-party providers — most of which are covered by Cloudflare's generous free tier and low-cost AI APIs. We never charge monthly subscriptions, never take a commission on orders, and never mark up third-party fees.

### 5.2 Pricing Strategy

We use a **one-time system build & deployment fee** model — not annual subscriptions. Rationale:

- Monthly operating costs are minimal ($0–0.50 CAD/mo base, plus payment processing fees), so there is no need to amortize infrastructure costs through recurring fees.
- Canadian small business owners overwhelmingly prefer one-time payments over ongoing commitments.
- AI chatbots are normally $29–$299/mo; our one-time fee matches 6–12 months of their SaaS fees but gives permanent ownership.
- Scope is well-defined (template → configure → deploy), making project-based pricing natural.

Our pricing is positioned aggressively below Canadian market averages:

## 9. Technology Architecture

The entire system runs on **Cloudflare's global edge network** — no servers to manage, automatic scaling, built-in global CDN, and 99.99% availability.

### Platform Stack

| Layer | Technology | Benefit |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Hosting | Cloudflare Pages | Global CDN, <1s first load, free |
| API | Cloudflare Workers | Edge computing, ms cold start |
| Database | Cloudflare D1 (SQLite) | 5GB free, automatic backups |
| Storage | Cloudflare R2 | 10GB free, S3-compatible, zero egress fees |
| Vector DB | Cloudflare Vectorize | Thousand-dimension vector search, beta free |
| AI Inference | Workers AI (Llama 3.1 8B) | Free within quota, edge-deployed |
| Embeddings | Workers AI (BGE Small EN 1.5) | Free within quota |
| Voice | Twilio Programmable Voice | Pay-per-minute, ~$0.018 CAD/min |
| Payments | Stripe / Square API | PCI-compliant, direct integration |

## 12. FAQ

**Q: Do I pay monthly or yearly?**
A: Neither. Our fee is a one-time payment for building and deploying your system. After that, you only pay third-party providers directly (domain ~$15/yr, hosting ~$99/yr from year 2, AI tokens ~$0.30–0.50/mo if applicable). No hidden fees.

**Q: What's included in the first year?**
A: The first year of Cloudflare Pages hosting is included in the build fee. From year 2 onward, hosting is ~$99/yr paid directly to Cloudflare. Domain registration (~$15/yr) is always paid to the domain registrar.

**Q: What if I have no technical background?**
A: No problem. We handle the entire setup — you just provide your menu, photos, and basic business info. After deployment, you manage daily operations through a simple staff dashboard.

**Q: Is the website bilingual?**
A: Yes. All Essential plans include Chinese/English bilingual websites and staff dashboards. This is a core feature designed for the Canadian ethnic restaurant market.

**Q: Can the AI give wrong answers?**
A: The AI responds strictly based on documents you upload (menu, policies, FAQs). It stays within the boundaries of your knowledge base. You can review conversation logs anytime from the dashboard.

**Q: Is payment processing secure?**
A: Yes. We integrate directly with Stripe and Square's official APIs. Payment data never touches our servers — transactions happen within the payment processor's PCI-compliant environment.

**Q: Can I upgrade later?**
A: Absolutely. Upgrade from Basic → Standard → Premium by paying the price difference. All your data (menu, orders, knowledge base) is preserved — no migration needed.

**Q: How does this compare to DoorDash/Uber Eats?**
A: Those platforms charge 25–30% commission per order. Our system costs $0/order — you only pay standard credit card processing fees (2.6–2.9%). On $9,000 monthly volume, that saves you over $2,250 CAD/month.

**Q: Do you offer support in Vancouver?**
A: Yes. We provide in-person consultation and training throughout Metro Vancouver. For merchants outside Vancouver, we offer remote setup and video training.

**Q: What if I need help after deployment?**
A: Optional annual maintenance ($800–$1,500/yr) covers security patches, content backups, and technical support. For Custom Enterprise, daily maintenance and dedicated support are included.
